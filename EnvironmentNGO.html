About ATREE

Ashoka Trust for Research in Ecology and the Environment (ATREE) is a research institution based in Bengaluru, India, dedicated to conducting interdisciplinary research, education, and outreach in the fields of ecology, environment, and sustainable development. Here's an overview of its information, mission, projects, and impact areas:


Mission of ATREE

  • ATREE's mission is to generate rigorous interdisciplinary knowledge for environmental conservation and sustainable development.
  • It aims to bridge the gap between research, policy, and practice by conducting scientific research that informs policy decisions and contributes to the development of sustainable solutions.


Projects of ATREE

  • ATREE is involved in a wide range of projects across various disciplines within ecology, environment, and sustainable development.s and contributes to the development of sustainable solutions.
  • Projects often involve collaboration with local communities, government agencies, non-governmental organizations (NGOs), and academic institutions to address pressing environmental challenges.


Impact Areas of ATREE

  • Biodiversity Conservation: ATREE conducts research on biodiversity hotspots, endangered species, and conservation strategies to protect India's rich biological diversity.
  • Sustainable Development: The organization works towards promoting sustainable livelihoods, natural resource management, and resilience-building among vulnerable communities.
  • Climate Change Adaptation: ATREE's research focuses on understanding the impacts of climate change on ecosystems and developing adaptation strategies to enhance resilience.

About Chintan Environmental Research

Chintan Environmental Research and Action Group is a non-profit organization based in India that focuses on environmental sustainability and social justice


Mission

  • Chintan's mission revolves around creating sustainable solutions to environmental challenges while addressing social injustices, particularly those affecting marginalized communities. The organization aims to promote environmental awareness, advocate for policy changes, and implement grassroots initiatives to improve waste management, reduce pollution, and enhance livelihood opportunities.


Projects

  • Waste Management: Chintan works extensively on waste management projects, particularly focusing on electronic waste (e-waste), plastic waste, and informal recycling sectors
  • Environmental Justice: The organization advocates for environmental justice, working to empower marginalized communities affected by environmental degradation and pollution.


Impact Areas

  • Improved Waste Management: By implementing innovative waste management practices and advocating for policy changes, Chintan has helped improve waste management systems in urban areas, reducing pollution and environmental degradation.
  • Empowerment of Informal Waste Workers: Chintan's efforts have led to improved working conditions and livelihood opportunities for informal waste workers, who often work in hazardous conditions without proper protection.
